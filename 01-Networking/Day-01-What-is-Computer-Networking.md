# 📡 What is Computer Networking?

## 📌 Definition:
Computer Networking is the process of connecting two or more computers or devices together to share data, resources, and information.

---

## 🔗 Key Points:
- It allows communication between devices  
- Helps in sharing files, printers, and internet  
- Can be wired (cables) or wireless (Wi-Fi)  
- Used in homes, offices, schools, and worldwide systems  

---

## 🌐 Why Networking is Important:
- Fast communication  
- Resource sharing  
- Centralized data management  
- Internet access  

---

## 🧩 Types of Networks:
- **LAN (Local Area Network):** Small area (home, office)  
- **WAN (Wide Area Network):** Large area (countries, internet)  
- **MAN (Metropolitan Area Network):** City-level network  

---

## 💡 Example:
When you use Wi-Fi at home to connect your phone and laptop to the internet, you are using a computer network.

---

## 🎯 Conclusion:
Computer Networking is the backbone of modern communication and plays a key role in connecting the world.
